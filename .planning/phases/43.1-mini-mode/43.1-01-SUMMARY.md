## Decisions Made
- Child visibility is bulk-toggled (all except joystickPad_) on compact entry; restored on Full return — resized() re-layouts via setBounds
- applyWindowMode sets constrainer BEFORE setSize to avoid aspect-ratio rejection of the new size
- savedUiScale_.store() moved inside the `if (windowMode_ == Full)` guard — compact mode setSize never overwrites the saved scale
- D-pad modes 3/4 fall through to the BPM/looper else-branch — no separate behavior needed for window-mode D-pad
- isPresetScrollActive() changed from `!= 0` to `== 1 || == 2` so modes 3/4 don't activate interval/octave highlight
