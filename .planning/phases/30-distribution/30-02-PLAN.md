    - Random Free + RND SYNC OFF: gates fire at truly irregular intervals via Poisson distribution — no two consecutive gates snap to the same tempo subdivision

    - Random Free + DAW Sync ON: gates follow the DAW beat grid (existing behavior, now clearly separated from the other modes)

    **Looper Visualization**
    - Horizontal progress strip replaced by a clockwise 2px green bar that travels around the outer perimeter of the Looper section — one full circuit equals one loop cycle
    - Ghost ring (1px, dimmed) is visible when the looper is stopped — the perimeter is always readable
    - "LOOPER" section label remains fully legible at all times — the bar passes alongside, never over, the label text
