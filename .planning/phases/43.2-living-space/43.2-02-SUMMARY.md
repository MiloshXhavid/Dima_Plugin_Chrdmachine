# Phase 43.2 Plan 02: Living Space Draw Passes Summary

**Four new paint() passes (hue drift, nebula blobs, star twinkle + fade-in, shooting star streak) plus INV co-rotation — starfield and background photo now spin as one unit during the 12s INV animation**

## Performance

- **Duration:** ~20 min
- **Started:** 2026-03-09T19:10:00Z
- **Completed:** 2026-03-09T19:30:00Z
- **Tasks:** 1 (+ 1 post-UAT enhancement)
- **Files modified:** 1

## Accomplishments

- Layer 1 background fill replaced with `fromHSV` hue-drift: ±8° over a 120s sine cycle, extremely subtle warm/cool oscillation
- Layer 2.5 nebula gas clouds inserted between milky way and starfield: 3 soft radial-gradient blobs (purple/teal at ~5% alpha), each ellipse-stretched via `AffineTransform::scale`
- Layer 3 starfield draw loop now modulates each star's alpha by `sin(twinklePhase)*0.10` (±10% brightness) and a 30-tick fade-in ramp — no more popping respawns
- Layer 3.5 shooting star streak: progress-based gradient `drawLine` with bell-shaped length envelope (fade-in 0→0.5, fade-out 0.7→1.0 using `jmap/jlimit` products)
- Post-UAT: INV co-rotation wraps entire starfield + shooting star in `ScopedSaveState` with `bgRotAngle_` rotation, and `driftHeading_` compensates each timer tick — stars and photo rotate as one locked unit during the 12s INV toggle animation

## Task Commits

Each task was committed atomically:

1. **Task 1: Insert hue drift, nebula, twinkle, and shooting star draw passes** - `3df7147` (feat)
2. **Post-UAT enhancement: INV co-rotation of starfield + shooting star** - `552a099` (feat)

## Files Created/Modified

- `Source/PluginEditor.cpp` — Four new/modified paint() layers (Layer 1 hue drift, Layer 2.5 nebulae, Layer 3 twinkle, Layer 3.5 shooting star); INV co-rotation `ScopedSaveState` wrap + `driftHeading_` compensation

## Decisions Made

- Nebula `effectiveAlpha = nb.alpha * (1 - warpT)` — consistent with CONTEXT.md warp table; clouds dissolve as warp ramps in
- Twinkle multiplier is `(1 + twinkleOff * warpFade)` not additive — modulation depth scales to zero at full warp while base star alpha is preserved
- Shooting star peak alpha = 0.5 (faint by design); fades with `(1 - warpT)`
- INV co-rotation wraps both Layer 3 and Layer 3.5 in a single `ScopedSaveState` — same rotation origin as background photo (`b.getCentreX(), b.getCentreY()`), so the entire space scene rotates as one unit
- `driftHeading_` compensation: per-tick delta of `bgRotAngle_` is subtracted from `driftHeading_` in `timerCallback` — pilot's heading is maintained in world-space even as the canvas rotates

## Deviations from Plan

### Post-UAT Enhancement (out-of-plan, user-initiated)

**INV co-rotation of starfield with background photo**

- **Identified after:** UAT approval of Task 1
- **Issue:** After UAT, user noticed that during INV toggle the background photo rotates 12° but stars continued drifting in screen-space — breaking the "single space scene" illusion
- **Fix:** Wrapped Layer 3 and Layer 3.5 in `ScopedSaveState` + `addTransform(AffineTransform::rotation(-bgRotAngle_rad, cx, cy))`; added `driftHeading_` compensation in `timerCallback` to prevent apparent heading change during rotation
- **Files modified:** `Source/PluginEditor.cpp`
- **Commit:** `552a099` (post-UAT, committed directly by user)
