<objective>
Add a bipolar attenuation slider to each Sister LFO row. When a cross-modulation destination
is selected the sister combo shrinks to 50% width and a horizontal -1..+1 slider appears in
the right 50%. "None" keeps the combo full-width and the slider hidden. Two new APVTS float
params persist the values. Negative atten inverts the sister signal; zero kills it entirely.

Purpose: gives the user per-channel control over cross-modulation depth and direction without
any popup or extra panel.

- Source/PluginProcessor.cpp (2 new params + 2 modified call sites)
